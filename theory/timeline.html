1. Research & Planning

State-of-the-Art assessment (IGA, ROM, Digital Twin). Software requirements definition and initial literature review.

2. Computational Core

Definition of 2D/3D parameterized test cases. Generating the ROM basis and validating the reduced model strategy.

3. Physical Prototypes

CAD design (FreeCAD/SolidWorks) and 3D printing of structural models for the digital twin set-up.

4. Digital Twin Integration

App UI development and real-time virtual environment scaling. Connecting physical and virtual set-ups with hybrid logic.

5. Finalization & Papers

Developing pedagogical tutorials and environments. Drafting final research papers and internship conclusions.
