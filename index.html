Project Overview

Innovative structural mechanics at LMSSC, Cnam.

This project focuses on **Reduced Order Modelling (ROM)** of nonlinear dynamic problems with large displacements and geometrical parameters. By leveraging **Isogeometric Analysis (IGA)** and advanced hyper-reduction techniques, we are constructing a methodology to solve complex structural mechanics problems in real-time.

The core research originality lies in creating a pedagogical "Digital Twin" set-up that integrates a physical 3D-printed model with a real-time virtual environment for FSI and non-linear dynamics courses.
